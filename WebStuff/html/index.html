Powerful analytics

We use late-night vehicular traffic to predict foot traffic at various late-night establishments. Business foot traffic and gross profit data are both notoriously hard to get for outsiders of the liquor distribution and supply business. Our analytics provide you with best-in-class estimates of vehicular traffic which can be used in your workflows to estimate this data and gain a commercial advantage.
